LightSDK.

Lua, a easy programming language, huh? Well; LightSDK improves that experience by giving you the free and private world of programming and giving you more tools at your fingertips. With the ROBLOX Studio core; publish your applications, and enjoy users using your application.

Unique.

Instead of creating a new Studio, we just created a studio from a studio. This core is efficient and improved by us, and got it to the best quality and performance for free. With advanced tools and simple tools, we were determined to get a high quality and simplistic way to develop.

Only Roblox application files?

If you don't like the ROBLOX open-source application library, you may publish your program into a Corona or X-Code project with a few clicks. Add more plugins simply, by going to the "App-Store" to enjoy downloadable extensions.

Distributors and License

Since the LightSDK is a open-source project, with a large tools for your fingertips. You can publish your application for no fees or anything! If you plan to distribute it online, however you must acquire a open-source license to protect your program.
